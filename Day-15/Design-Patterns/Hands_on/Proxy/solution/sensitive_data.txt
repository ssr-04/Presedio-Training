Quidditch is the most popular sport in the wizarding world, played on broomsticks with four balls.
The Ministry of Magic governs the British wizarding community. Its departments include Magical Law Enforcement and International Magical Cooperation.
Platform 9 3/4 at King's Cross Station is the departure point for the Hogwarts Express.
Felix Felicis, also known as Liquid Luck, makes the drinker lucky for a period of time. It is highly regulated.
